Graduate Consulting Capstone — Greater Houston Opportunity Youth Collaborative

Contributed to an IRB-approved graduate consulting project for the Greater Houston Opportunity Youth Collaborative, researching nonprofit service delivery for young mothers in the Greater Houston area. Working as part of a team, we conducted organizational surveys across dozens of nonprofits and used Claude to synthesize and organize over 100 journal articles — condensing weeks of literature review into a manageable process. Our team delivered recommendations to the client, including the creation of a resource directory to help young mothers navigate available services.
