Meta-Story

How I used Claude to build this

This portfolio is itself a demonstration of something I believe deeply: that nonprofits — and the people who work in them — shouldn't be held back by unfamiliarity with technology. I used Claude — Anthropic's AI — to design, code, and deploy this site without writing a single line of code. Here's exactly how it worked.

01
Brief & Structure
I described my background and goals to Claude. Together we mapped out the ideal page structure and content strategy to showcase nonprofit experience for a professional audience.
02
Design & Code
Claude generated the full HTML, CSS, and multi-page architecture — a warm, responsive design with a custom color palette, typography, and interactive elements. I didn't write a single line from scratch.
03
Content Strategy
Claude helped me think through what to say on each page — not just the format, but the story: how to frame my experience, values, and vision in a way that's clear and authentic.
04
Deployment
Claude walked me through publishing the site for free on GitHub Pages — from creating the repo to going live with a custom URL. No expensive hosting needed.
05
Ongoing Review & Ethics
Using AI well means staying in the driver's seat. Throughout this project I reviewed every output for accuracy, corrected content that misrepresented my experience, and removed anything that didn't reflect my actual voice. Claude is a powerful collaborator — but the judgment, the values, and the accountability are always mine.
